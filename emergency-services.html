Frequently Asked Questions

Common Questions

Answers about emergency response for homes, HOAs, and managed properties.

What counts as an emergency maintenance issue?

Water leaks, storm damage, structural concerns, and other urgent issues that put a property or its occupants at risk qualify for emergency response.

How fast can CS Services be on-site?

Our target is to have a team on-site for an initial assessment within approximately one hour of an emergency call, availability permitting.

Is emergency response available for HOAs and condominiums?

Yes. Emergency response is available 24/7 for homes, HOAs, condominium associations, and managed properties across South Florida.
